Description

A boss rush, semi-bullet-hell game about a mother thats loses his son for the ocean and will do evething to get him back, inspired in Titan Souls and Enter the Gungeon.

History

On a small island, men have the right to sail, while women are left with only a narrow strip of land, their homes and their offspring. However, when her son disappears, a mother demonstrates all her determination and courage, facing sacrifices and trials to get him back. In this search, she will go against all the laws of men and gods, and will meet other transgressors like her, on a journey not only for her son, but also for freedom and belonging.

know more in the history page (PT-BR)

Features

  • Epic battles with unique 4 main bosses and 1 secret boss.
  • Explore small hand-crafted island with the secrets
  • A unique gameplay experience for bullet-hell fans
